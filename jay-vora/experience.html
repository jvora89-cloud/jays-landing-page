From Engineering to Entrepreneurship

Jay's journey began in software testing at Motorola, where he gained foundational experience in quality assurance and product development. He then advanced to a software engineering role at Lucent Technologies in 1998, working on telecommunications systems and deepening his technical expertise.

This combination of hands-on engineering experience at two major technology companies provided the technical foundation and industry insight necessary to launch Flextron Circuit Assembly in 2005.

Core Competencies

Product Management • Electronics Manufacturing • Product Marketing • Start-ups • Quality Systems • Telecommunications

Educational Background

Jay's formal education includes a Bachelor of Engineering in Electronics & Communications from Dharmsinh Desai University and a Master's degree focused in Telecommunications from Southern Methodist University, combining theoretical knowledge with practical application.

Throughout his career, Jay has demonstrated the ability to bridge technical expertise with business leadership, building Flextron into a respected name in contract electronics manufacturing while maintaining the hands-on technical insight that drives innovation.
