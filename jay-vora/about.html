About

Jay Vora is an experienced entrepreneur with a demonstrated history of working in the electrical and electronic manufacturing industry. As the Founder and CEO of Flextron Circuit Assembly, he has built a successful contract electronics manufacturing company serving industries from aerospace to medical devices.

Engineering Excellence

Building on a foundation of software engineering and telecommunications expertise to create innovative manufacturing solutions.

Based in Wood Dale, Illinois, Jay founded Flextron Circuit Assembly in 2005 after gaining valuable experience at major technology companies including Motorola and Lucent Technologies. His vision was to create a full-service contract electronics manufacturer that could meet the exacting standards of aerospace, medical, and telecommunications industries.

Education

Jay holds a Bachelor of Engineering in Electronics & Communications from Dharmsinh Desai University (1987-1991) and a Master's degree in Telecommunications from Southern Methodist University (1998-2000).

Expertise

His areas of expertise include Product Management, Electronics Manufacturing, Product Marketing, and building successful start-ups in the high-tech manufacturing sector. This combination of technical knowledge and business acumen has been instrumental in establishing Flextron as a trusted partner for complex electronic assembly projects.

Under his leadership, Flextron Circuit Assembly has achieved multiple industry certifications including Aerospace AS9100, Medical ISO 13485, ISO 9001, and ITAR registration, demonstrating the company's commitment to quality and compliance across regulated industries.
