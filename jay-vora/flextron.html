Flextron Circuit Assembly

Founded in 2005, Flextron Circuit Assembly is a full-service contract electronics manufacturer specializing in design, fabrication, and assembly of printed circuit boards. The company operates as a one-stop shop for complex electronic manufacturing needs.

We Engineer and Build for You

Flextron's mission centers on delivering precision manufacturing solutions for industries where quality and reliability are non-negotiable.

Location & Capabilities

Centrally located in Wood Dale, Illinois, just outside O'Hare International Airport, Flextron serves clients across multiple industries with specialized expertise in rigid and flexible printed circuit board assemblies of all types and technologies.

$9.3M Annual Revenue
22 Employees
2005 Founded

Services

Flextron offers the full range of manufacturing services in flexible and rigid printed circuit board assembly, including high-tech surface mount technology (SMT), through-hole (PTH), and mixed-technology boards. The company handles projects from initial design through final assembly.

Industries Served

The company serves a diverse range of sectors including military and defense, medical devices, lighting systems, and telecommunications infrastructure. Each industry benefits from Flextron's commitment to quality and precision manufacturing.

Certifications & Standards

  • Aerospace AS9100 Certified
  • Medical ISO 13485 Certified
  • ISO 9001 Certified
  • ITAR Registered

These certifications demonstrate Flextron's ability to meet the stringent quality and compliance requirements of highly regulated industries, ensuring that every product meets or exceeds industry standards.
